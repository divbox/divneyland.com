About Divneyland

Divneyland is a family photo archive — a place to collect and preserve memories across generations without relying on social media or third-party platforms that may not be around forever.

Photos are hosted on Cloudinary and described in a simple manifest file. The site itself is plain HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — no frameworks, no build step, no accounts required to view it.

Why "Divneyland"?

It's a family thing. 🎢

How photos get added

Photos are processed on a Mac (converted, resized, tagged) and uploaded via a local Node script. Each photo gets a manifest entry before it appears on the site. Nothing is uploaded directly from a phone.
